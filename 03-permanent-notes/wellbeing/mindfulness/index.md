## Key Practices

### Breathing as a Tool

Paced breathing is a foundational practice that:
- Activates the parasympathetic (rest and digest) nervous system
- Increases emotional control and awareness
- Helps you respond (not react) under pressure
- Balances emotional brain activity

### Core Principle

Regular practice during calm moments builds resilience so you can access these techniques during stressful times.

## Recommended Practice

- Practice daily, even when relaxed
- Consistency builds emotional resilience
- Start with 5-10 minutes
- Experiment with different breathing patterns
- Use as needed during stress or before high-pressure situations
