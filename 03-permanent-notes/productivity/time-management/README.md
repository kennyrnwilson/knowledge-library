## Key Principles

### Strategic Time Management Skills

1. **Prioritizing** - Identify and focus on what matters most
2. **Time Blocking** - Schedule specific blocks for all important activities
3. **Focusing** - Deep, undistracted work on cognitively demanding tasks
4. **Following Through** - Execution and consistency over planning
5. **Energizing** - Sustainable effort without burnout

### The Deep Life Stack

A sequential approach to personal transformation:

1. **Discipline** - Establish efficacy and capability
2. **Values** - Define core truths and principles
3. **Calm** - Gain control through systems and organization
4. **Planning** - Make intentional life changes

### Ultradian Rhythms

- 90-minute cycles of alertness and focus
- First 10-15 min = warm-up
- Next ~60 min = peak deep focus
- Ideal: 1-2 blocks per day, consistently
- Recovery and sleep consolidate learning
