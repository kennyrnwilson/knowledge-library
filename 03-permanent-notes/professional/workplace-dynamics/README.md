## Quick Principles

1. **Authenticity builds trust** - Be genuine and transparent
2. **Listen more than you speak** - Understand others' perspectives first
3. **Build relationships before you need them** - Networking is ongoing, not transactional
4. **Assume positive intent** - Most people aren't trying to sabotage you
5. **Focus on shared goals** - Alignment on objectives solves most conflicts
