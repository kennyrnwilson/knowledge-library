## Quick Principles

1. **Money is a tool, not a goal** - Use it to enable your actual goals
2. **Understand what you're investing in** - Don't invest in things you don't understand
3. **Diversification reduces risk** - Don't put all eggs in one basket
4. **Compound interest is powerful** - Start early, stay consistent
5. **Financial literacy is essential** - Know your numbers and options
