## Quick Principles

1. **Curation aids enjoyment** - Having a curated list helps you make better choices
2. **Diverse media feeds imagination** - Reading, watching, and experiencing varied content expands perspective
3. **Quality over quantity** - One great book beats ten mediocre ones
4. **Recommendations help** - Learning what others enjoyed can improve your choices
5. **Recording matters** - Tracking what you've consumed helps you remember and share
