## Key Principles

1. **Regularity** is most important - consistent sleep/wake times
2. **Quantity** target: 7-9 hours per night
3. **Quality** influenced by light, temperature, caffeine management
4. **Timing** varies by chronotype (early bird vs. night owl)

## Quick Action Items

- Set consistent bedtime/wake time
- Keep bedroom at 18°C (64°F)
- Get morning light exposure
- Dim lights 1 hour before bed
- Cut caffeine 10 hours before bed
- Move your body daily
