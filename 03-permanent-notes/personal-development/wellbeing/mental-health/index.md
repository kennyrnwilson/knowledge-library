## Key Areas

### Understanding Mental Strength

Mental health practices focus on:
- Stress management and response
- Emotional regulation and awareness
- Building psychological resilience
- Developing coping strategies
- Managing anxiety and difficult emotions

### Core Practices

- [Paced Breathing](../mindfulness/paced-breathing.md) - Nervous system regulation
- Meditation and mindfulness
- Cognitive reframing
- Journaling and reflection
- Social connection and support

## Key Principles

1. **Mental resilience can be built** - like physical fitness, it requires consistent practice
2. **Prevention is easier than crisis management** - build practices before stress peaks
3. **Stress response is trainable** - you can improve how you respond to adversity
4. **Connection matters** - relationships buffer against stress
5. **Recovery is active** - it requires sleep, movement, and mental downtime
