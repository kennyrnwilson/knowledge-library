# Weekly Reviews

**Purpose:** Regular weekly reflection on progress, habits, blockers, and planning for the coming week.

## What Goes Here

- Weekly reflection notes
- Progress on current goals
- Habit tracking summary
- Wins and learnings
- Blockers and adjustments
- Planning for next week

## Weekly Review Process

1. **Review** - Look at past week's progress
2. **Assess** - What went well? What didn't?
3. **Learn** - Extract insights
4. **Plan** - Set priorities for next week
