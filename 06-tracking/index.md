# Tracking & Reviews

**Purpose:** Track goals, habits, progress, and reflect through regular reviews at multiple time scales.

## What Goes Here

- Current goals and objectives
- Habit tracking and streaks
- Weekly review notes
- Monthly assessments
- Annual reflections

## How It Works

1. **Set** - Define goals in SMART terms
2. **Track** - Record progress regularly
3. **Review** - Weekly, monthly, annual assessments
4. **Adjust** - Update goals based on learnings

## Review Cycles

### Daily
- Track habits and streaks
- Log quick wins
- Note blockers

### Weekly
- Review progress on goals
- Assess habit compliance
- Plan next week
- [See Weekly Reviews](./weekly-reviews/index.md)

### Monthly
- Evaluate goal progress
- Adjust habits if needed
- Identify patterns
- Plan next month

### Annual
- Major reflection on year
- Celebrate wins
- Learn from setbacks
- Set next year's direction
- [See Annual Reviews](./annual-reviews/index.md)
