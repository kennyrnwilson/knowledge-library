# Annual Reviews

**Purpose:** Major yearly reflection on growth, achievements, learnings, and direction for the coming year.

## What Goes Here

- Year-end reflections
- Achievements and wins
- Challenges overcome
- Learnings and growth
- Next year's priorities
- Vision and goals

## Annual Review Process

1. **Celebrate** - Recognize your wins
2. **Reflect** - What did you learn?
3. **Evaluate** - Did you meet your goals?
4. **Plan** - Set direction for next year
5. **Commit** - Document your intentions

## What to Assess

- **Goals** - Which goals did you achieve?
- **Growth** - How have you evolved?
- **Relationships** - Impact on people in your life
- **Health** - Physical and mental wellbeing
- **Career** - Professional development
- **Finances** - Financial progress
- **Skills** - New capabilities gained
- **Mindset** - Changes in thinking

## Review Sections

Each annual review typically includes:
- Wins and achievements
- Challenges and how you overcame them
- Key learnings
- Growth areas
- Next year's focus
- Goals for coming year
