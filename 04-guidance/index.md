# Guidance

**Purpose:** Blueprints and operating procedures for how you run your life. Standard operating procedures, frameworks, methodologies, routines, workflows, decision-making guides, and checklists.

**Think of this as your personal operations manual.**

## What Goes Here

- Standard operating procedures (SOPs)
- Frameworks and methodologies
- Routines and workflows
- Decision-making guides
- Checklists and quick reference guides
- Best practices by area of life

## How It Works

1. **Create** - Develop procedures and frameworks as you identify patterns
2. **Refine** - Update based on experience and results
3. **Use** - Reference when facing recurring situations
4. **Review** - Periodically optimize your procedures
