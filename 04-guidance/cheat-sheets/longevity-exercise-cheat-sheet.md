# Longevity Exercise Cheat Sheet

**Purpose:** 9 core functional movements for long-term health, strength, and mobility.

## Core Functional Movements (6)

### 1. Overhead Press
- **Benefits:** Shoulder strength, core stability, shoulder health
- **Form:** Press weight from shoulder height to full elbow extension overhead
- **Tip:** Maintain neutral spine, engage core throughout

### 2. Pull-ups
- **Benefits:** Back strength, upper body pulling power, grip strength
- **Form:** Full range of motion from dead hang to chin over bar
- **Tip:** Control the descent, avoid momentum-based reps

### 3. Seal Rows
- **Benefits:** Back thickness, rowing pattern, upper back strength
- **Form:** Rowing motion while maintaining horizontal body position
- **Tip:** Focus on scapular retraction at peak contraction

### 4. Push-ups
- **Benefits:** Chest, shoulders, triceps, core stability
- **Form:** Full range from extended arms to chest touching ground
- **Tip:** Keep elbows at 45-degree angle to body

### 5. Goblet Squats
- **Benefits:** Leg strength, mobility, core engagement, posterior chain
- **Form:** Hold weight at chest, squat to full depth
- **Tip:** Chest up, knees tracking over toes

### 6. Lunges (Forward or Reverse)
- **Benefits:** Unilateral leg strength, balance, hip mobility
- **Form:** Step forward/backward, lower until back knee nearly touches ground
- **Tip:** Maintain upright torso, front knee stable

## Supplemental Movements (3)

### 7. Lateral Raises
- **Benefits:** Shoulder width, lateral deltoid development
- **Form:** Raise arms to shoulder height with slight elbow bend
- **Tip:** Control the weight, avoid momentum

### 8. Barbell Curls
- **Benefits:** Bicep strength, arm development
- **Form:** Curl weight from full extension to chest
- **Tip:** Minimize elbow movement, strict form preferred

### 9. Rope Extensions
- **Benefits:** Tricep strength, arm development
- **Form:** Extend rope downward against cable resistance
- **Tip:** Full extension, controlled return

## Training Structure

- **Strength Focus:** 3-5 sets of 3-8 reps
- **Hypertrophy Focus:** 3-4 sets of 6-12 reps
- **Endurance Focus:** 2-3 sets of 12-20 reps

## Key Principles

1. **Consistency** - Regular training matters more than perfection
2. **Progressive Overload** - Gradually increase weight or volume
3. **Recovery** - Allow adequate rest between training sessions
4. **Full Range of Motion** - Use complete movement patterns
5. **Mobility** - Maintain joint health with stretching and mobility work
