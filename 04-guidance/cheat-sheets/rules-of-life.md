# Rules of Life

**Purpose:** Personal operating principles and guidelines for decision-making and behavior.

## Your Rules

*Add your personal rules of life here - principles that guide your decisions and actions across all areas of life.*

## Framework for Creating Rules

### What Makes a Good Rule?

1. **Clear and Specific** - Easy to understand and apply
2. **Universal** - Applies across different situations
3. **Actionable** - Something you can actually do
4. **Meaningful** - Reflects your values
5. **Tested** - Based on experience, not just theory

### Example Categories

- **Relationships:** How you treat others, boundaries, communication
- **Work:** Professional standards, decision-making, time investment
- **Health:** Sleep, exercise, nutrition non-negotiables
- **Money:** Spending, saving, investment principles
- **Learning:** How you approach growth and knowledge
- **Time:** What deserves your attention and focus
- **Character:** Core values and ethical standards

## How to Use This

1. **Reflect** - What principles have served you well?
2. **Extract** - Identify the underlying rules from your best decisions
3. **Document** - Write them clearly in your own words
4. **Apply** - Reference when facing important decisions
5. **Revise** - Update as you learn and grow
